Education is the most powerful weapon which you can use to change the world.

Nelson Mandela

Even if Mandela himself had not penned these powerful words we all know this to be intrinsically true. What sets children apart from others is their access to a quality education, where the focus on the development of the child centers on their exposure to opportunities that set them apart from others. In this rapidly changing world the ability to move with the driving forces that are shaping our world are essential for future success. To develop skills in students that will serve them well into next century, and as they step out beyond the classroom ready to engage with real life, British Education Korea sets out to do all that.

At British Education Korea, we strive to teach girls and boys from eighteen months of age the skills they need to be successful adults. We espouse to see them develop into confident, capable and globally minded individuals who believe in a future they are part of. We at British Education Korea build character by articulating and demonstrating it, we engage students in lessons where they are encouraged to enquire, we ignite a passion of lifelong learning and we have standards that are benchmarked against international standards.

At British Education Korea, it’s not necessarily about being the brightest student. It’s about students who have the temperament to do their best, to work the hardest, to persevere at a task and follow it through to the end in a responsible manner. British Education Korea takes the education of every child seriously. Our UK based curriculum is varied, exciting and holistic and we work in partnership with our families to create a curriculum that stimulates both hearts and minds.

Visitors are always welcome to drop in and see us in action. The doors are always wide open and we welcome everyone for one simple reason: we are proud of what we are achieving at British Education Korea.

Jennifer Mills

About Us

The British Prep, Gangnam House is a co education facility educating students from 5 years old and above.

Located in central Seoul, the focus is on preparing students to enter the secondary school of their choice with a rigorous academic structure suited to older students.

   


We are proud of what we do and are always keen to show off our facilities! Please don't hesitate to get in touch with us if you have any questions or you would like to arrange a visit.
