The British Education Korea Group

The British Education Korea Group currently operates across four sites in Seoul offering the English National Curriculum, with some adaptations to suit the local environment, including Mandarin for all children from nursery upwards. Places are currently offered to students from 18 months up to Year 8.

Each campus has its own distinctive ethos which, with the support of the local community, has evolved over time. However, all our locations share our common aims and values. The group encourages every child to be the best they can be with support and guidance offered at all stages of each individual student journey to the senior school of their choice.

We firmly believe that effective partnerships with parents are critical in developing each child’s leadership, confidence and creativity, as well as a strong academic performance. This is all achieved by recruiting excellent teaching staff who place the welfare of the children at the heart of everything they do. The ultimate aim is to ensure that every child in our care is happy and has the absolute best possible start in life in preparation for a happy and successful future in the 21st Century.

Nigel Venning

Director of British Education Korea

Preparatory Education

Education is about much more than studying and passing exams. At British Education Korea we believe that our children should experience a unique character-moulding journey, a ‘preparation’ for the next stage of their lives.

As well as entry to excellent senior schools in Korea, Asia and the UK, we will help our pupils develop the tools needed to meet the challenges of the working world and with the confidence to address the inevitable challenges they will face.

   

Our Teachers

We believe that teachers matter. Teachers educate us on far more than history, mathematics, art, music, or English. They teach us about hard work and the importance of doing the best we can in all that we do. They inspire us to pursue our dreams and give us the tools and support we need to succeed.
