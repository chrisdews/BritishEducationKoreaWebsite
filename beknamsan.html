Welcome to The British Lodge, Namsan House!

We are fortunate to have purpose-built facilities and to be in the heart of the exciting and dynamic city of Seoul. Our indoor and outdoor learning spaces provide children with the opportunity to interact with and appreciate nature. This is designed to help the children develop a deep understanding of everyone’s role to care for our world. In our programme design, we recognize that an integrated community of diverse people is fundamental to a successful education; great facilities and curriculum attracts committed and experienced teachers who are passionately attached to molding young minds and to serving their community.

Our teachers come from a variety of countries, bringing with them substantial early years and international teaching experience. Our faculty seeks to nurture and develop the 'whole child' by providing experiences that enhance a child’s cognitive and affective domains. Our teachers are responsive to children’s unique abilities and they design plans for the individual learning needs of our children. We believe that what defines our early years program is the ability to link the best teachers with a responsive curriculum, in a caring environment.

We recognize that children who have a positive start to the their formal learning experience are more likely to feel comfortable, valued, and have a strong and positive attitude towards lifelong learning. Choosing a campus with a specialized early years programme is one of the most important decisions parents will make for their children. At the British Lodge, Namsan House, we understand that a positive transition to an early years’ environment impacts how a child successfully engages with their future learning. We encourage our parents to be actively involved in their child’s education and learning through our friendly and welcoming community.

Thank you for taking the time to learn more about The British Lodge. Do please contact us for a visit soon!

About Us

The British Lodge, Namsan House is a purpose built facility with a focus on pre-school aged children aged 18 months and above.

Located in the shadow of the North Seoul Tower, next to the Grand Hyatt Hotel Seoul, from here most students will transition to The British Prep, Gangnam House. The curriculum is designed around inquiry based learning.

 


We are proud of what we do and are always keen to show off our facilities! Please don't hesitate to get in touch with us if you have any questions or you would like to arrange a visit.
