Welcome to The British Prep Blog site!

This site is dedicated to communicating with parents and is designed to be a place where we blog about what’s happening in our school. Please take some time to see what’s happening in our school and check out your child’s class for the latest information. Your child’s day is a busy one! They learn, they think, they create, they collaborate, they sing, run, jump and they make…and now we have a place to share their day with you. Technology gives us the tools to make a real links between home and school and our Blog is the perfect forum to connect us all together. Please join in on your child’s learning journey.
Kind regards
Jennifer Mills
